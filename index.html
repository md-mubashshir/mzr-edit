Editing Apps

Light Room

Lightroom offers a wide range of editing tools that allow users to adjust exposure, contrast, color balance, and other aspects of their photos. It also includes features such as noise reduction, lens correction, and sharpening to help enhance image quality. Additionally, Lightroom allows users to create and apply presets, which are pre-defined editing settings that can be applied to multiple photos for a consistent look.

Adobe Photoshop

Alight Motion is a video editing and motion graphics app that is available for Android and iOS devices. It allows users to create professional-quality animations, motion graphics, visual effects, video editing, and video compositing.

Pixellab

PixelLab is a text and image editing app that is available for Android and iOS devices. It allows users to create and edit images with text, stickers, and various effects. The app is designed to be user-friendly and accessible to people with little or no design experience.
